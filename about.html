USC Math Club

USC Math Club's purpose is to promote an interest in math among our diverse student body as well as to bridge bonds of friendship between math and non-math majors alike. We organize events for our members every semester. These events include an undergraduate math research panel, an info session on graduate school applications, a career panel, trips to local math conferences, and of course our beginning of the semester BBQs. We are open to everybody, regardless of major. We have no requirements or application to join, and anyone is welcome to attend our events. If you'd like to be kept in the loop about what we're doing, sign up using the button below.

If you have any questions, you can contact us at uscmathclub@gmail.com.
